Today's action

As outdoor enthusiasts, we seek out lands such as these to evoke feelings of solitude, joy, amazement, and on and on. It is up to us to become ambassadors. Who better to speak to others about what one can feel in these places? To know how your life can be touched? To value the lives of many creatures and plants amongst the rocks and waters and snowbanks.

Today, we will go beyond the circle of like-minded friends, and reach out to share our perspective with those who don't yet understand.

Today, we give voice to the National Monuments under review by Interior Secretary Ryan Zinke. All it takes is saying why these places matter to you.

About Us

Hi! I'm Anil. After moving to the heart of the Pacific Northwest in Seattle, I found myself at home. Nestled in the city of Seattle, but with quick escapes to rainforests, alpine lakes, and friendships in the outdoor community. I started this space to be ours - by folks who care about the Earth, for us.
